4. Material Specifications

The housing assembly shall be fabricated from Grade 304 stainless steel with a minimum wall thickness of 2.0 mm. Surface finish shall comply with Ra ≤ 1.6 µm on all sealing faces.

Operating temperature range: -20°C to +85°C. Storage: -40°C to +100°C. Humidity per IEC 60068-2-78.
